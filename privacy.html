Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

We do not collect any personal data about your Bitcoin transactions, wallet addresses, or usage of the calculator tools. All portfolio data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and never sent to us.

Cookies

We use cookies for Google AdSense (ads) and analytics. You may accept or reject cookies through our cookie banner.

Third-Party Services

We may use Google AdSense and Google Analytics. These services may use cookies to deliver relevant ads and measure site performance.

Your Rights

You can opt out of cookies, unsubscribe from email alerts, and request removal of your email data at any time.
