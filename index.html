FAQ

What is Satoshi Calculator?

It is a Bitcoin tool suite: live price, converter, profit & DCA calculators, portfolio, chart, news, and more.

Which APIs power the site?

Prices: CoinGecko with fallback to Binance/Kraken. Chart: TradingView. News: RSS via proxy with curated fallback.

Is my data private?

Portfolio entries are stored locally in your browser (localStorage). We don’t collect personal data.

How often are prices updated?

Every 5 seconds with retries and cached last-known price for reliability.

Why only Bitcoin?

We specialize in premium Bitcoin-focused tools to keep the experience fast and high quality.

When is the next Bitcoin halving?

The next halving is expected around 2028 at block height 1,050,000.

Can I use this tool on mobile?

Yes! The site is fully responsive and works on mobile and desktop.
